SOUL OF ATHENS PRESENTS
MEDICAL ACCESS

Residents of the Athens County community are struggling to get the medical help they need, whether it includes going to a hospital, seeing specialists or even a simple check up. Our team is going to explore the medical access issues, such as those previously mentioned, in Athens County by identifying the most prevelant aspects based off feedback from professionals. We will pick a limited amount of issues and represent them by a person or family that deals with these struggles and the perspective of a medical professional.
